The Band of the Gloucestershire Constabulary is pleased to anounce the release of our latest CD, A Night at the Movies.

The band's new CD invites you to enjoy a night at the movies with a collection of tunes from classic films of the last 70 years.

The CD costs £5 (UK postage £2) and is available at all of our concerts or please contact us at sales@glospoliceband.org">sales@glospoliceband.org.
